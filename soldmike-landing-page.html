Who I Am

I'm Michael Barillari — Real Estate Broker with RE/MAX Premier, and the person behind SOLDMIKE. I didn't start in real estate. I understand construction, materials, and quality of work — and I bring that eye to every listing I take on.

Today, I'm also a professional photographer and videographer. I don't outsource the most important part of selling your home — I handle it personally, because presentation is everything and I won't cut corners on it.
