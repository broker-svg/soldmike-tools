The Property

Thoughtfully Upgraded.
Move-In Ready.

Tucked on a quiet, family-friendly street in Vaughan, 179 Lio Ave delivers over 3,000 sq ft of thoughtfully upgraded living — plus a fully finished basement with a separate entrance ready for extended family or rental income. The main floor showcases an upgraded kitchen with crown moulding, GE Café gas range, premium hooded fan, and a full-size fridge with water and ice. Plaster crown moulding and trim dress the family and dining rooms. Five bedrooms, four upgraded bathrooms, California shutters on the main floor, wooden blinds throughout the upper — every detail considered. New furnace, AC, and water tank already replaced. Outside: stone stairs, custom cast iron railings, jewel stone porch, new fence, extended interlocking driveway, professionally landscaped yard, half-moon patio, and an aluminum gazebo. The 2-car garage features a custom overhead mezzanine and new insulated door. Gas line roughed in for the backyard BBQ. Minutes to Hwy 400, top schools, and every amenity you need.
