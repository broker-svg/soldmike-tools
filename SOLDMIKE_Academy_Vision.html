Why webinars don't work anymore — and what does.

The Business Model

Why yearly subscription wins.

Pay-as-you-go sounds flexible, but it turns the Academy into a transaction instead of a relationship. Yearly subscription is what builds a real business — predictable revenue, compounding value, and genuine commitment from members.

Pay-As-You-Go

Transaction-based
~$99
Per course
Low
Commitment
  • Unpredictable revenue
  • No community buy-in
  • Members leave after one purchase
  • Constantly chasing new buyers
  • Race to the bottom on pricing

Yearly Subscription

Relationship-based · Our pick
$1,997
Per year
High
Commitment
  • Predictable, recurring revenue
  • Members treat it like tuition
  • Built-in community & accountability
  • All tracks included — more perceived value
  • Continuous updates keep it fresh

The math at 100 members

At $1,997/year, 100 members = $199,700/year in recurring revenue. That's a real business with a real moat. Pay-as-you-go at $99/course would require 2,000+ individual purchases to hit the same number — with zero guarantee anyone comes back. And if we hit 500 members at year three? ~$1M/year recurring. That's the difference between a side project and a platform.

The Path

How we build and launch.

We don't need 12 months to go live. If we focus, we can have a minimum viable Academy running within 90 days and a full platform within 6. Here's how it sequences.

1

Foundation

Days 0–90 · Build the core
Lock the positioning, price, and audience. Build the platform backbone and launch Track 1 (Real Estate) as the flagship.
  • Define member persona and promise
  • Select platform (Kajabi, Circle, Skool, or custom)
  • Build Real Estate track (10–12 core modules)
  • Set up community space and onboarding flow
  • Record flagship content with our existing gear
2

Soft Launch

Days 90–180 · Founding members
Invite a closed group of 25–50 founding members at a discount. Build in public, ship improvements weekly, collect real testimonials.
  • Founding member pricing ($997 year one)
  • Launch Marketing + Video Production tracks
  • Weekly live sessions to build community
  • Capture wins, testimonials, case studies
  • Iterate content based on member feedback
3

Full Launch

Days 180–365 · Scale
Public launch at full pricing. All 6 tracks live. Ongoing content production. Marketing engine turned on across channels.
  • Launch Finance, Investing, Content tracks
  • Pricing moves to $1,997/year
  • Paid ads, partnerships, affiliate program
  • Monthly masterclasses with guest experts
  • Target 100 active members by year one
4

Scale

Year 2+ · Expand the moat
Add premium tier, brokerage partnerships, and a certification program. Become the industry-standard education platform.
  • Premium tier ($4,997/year) with 1:1 coaching
  • Brokerage partnerships (bulk licences)
  • SOLDMIKE Certified program
  • Live events and retreats
  • Target 500+ members year three
